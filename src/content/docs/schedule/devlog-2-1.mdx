        The video explained how computers work using a simple transistor which switches inputs from either on or off (1 or 0). Using this a computer thinks only in yes or no. Humans on the other hand don't need to think in only yes or no's but because we attempt to do so it causes issues of making binary out of complex things. This leads to things like having right and left brains being creative and analytical when that's not the truth, a brain is both analytical and creative and everyone has th capablities of doing either.
